What is Bitcoin ?

Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer electronic cash system that enables direct online payments without relying on financial institutions. It addresses the double-spending problem by using a decentralized network that timestamps transactions through a chain of hash-based proof-of-work. The longest chain represents the valid transaction history and reflects the majority of computing power, ensuring security as long as most participants act honestly. The system requires minimal structure, allowing nodes to freely join and leave the network while relying on the longest chain as the authoritative record.
